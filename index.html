Welcome to the Pig Dice web game!

"Pig Dice is a simple dice game played between two people. Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled (which makes the player lose their score that turn) or the player decides to "hold" (which allows a player to keep their turn score but ends their turn). The first player to score 100 or more points wins." - Wikipedia
